Welcome To
Absolute Shawarma

Shawarma, the Middle Eastern delicacy, is a beloved favorite that wins hearts wherever it goes. At Absolute Shawarma, we bring this delicious experience to you, no matter which part of India you are in. Our shawarmas feature gently grilled juicy meats and fillings, perfectly complemented by the creaminess, flavor, and acidity of Tahini, chili sauce, and garlic mayonnaise. These are paired with fresh, crunchy salad veggies, all wrapped up in the warm, soft embrace of Lebanese bread.

It's not just a meal; it's an experience that makes your day better. As die-hard foodies know, a great meal makes a great day! At Absolute Shawarma, we offer not one, not two, but four delicious shawarma options: Chicken and Paneer. Each bite promises a treat for your taste buds

The Brand

In 2017, Absolute Shawarma has blossomed from a small unit in Bangalore into a culinary powerhouse, today it has over 100 outlets spread across 30 dynamic cities throughout India. Visitors to Absolute Shawarma's outlets are greeted with a diverse menu that goes far beyond the boundaries of Traditional shawarmas.

What truly sets Absolute Shawarma apart, however, is their relentless pursuit of perfection in every dish. Each recipe undergoes a process of development and testing, ensuring that every bite is an explosion of flavour and authentic and healthy. From sourcing the finest ingredients to refining cooking techniques, No detail is overlooked in their quest to deliver culinary excellence.

Whether you're a shawarma enthusiast or an adventurous foodie looking to explore new tastes, Absolute Shawarma invites you to join them on a journey of flavor and discovery.

Come and experience firsthand the passion, quality, and innovation that have made Absolute Shawarma the ultimate destination for food lovers across India."
