About Us

-Our Story

Absolute Shawarma a Shawarma Franchise is a rapidly growing food brand that started as a small unit in Bangalore in 2017. Over the course of 7 years, the company has expanded its presence to over 100+ outlets in 30+ cities, becoming a well-known name in the food industry. Their remarkable growth can be attributed to the quality and appeal of their diverse menu offerings.

Absolute Shawarma Unveils a Versatile Menu

While specializing in Shawarmas, Absolute Shawarma has gone above and beyond to meet customer demands by introducing a range of other items such as Momos, rolls, chicken Shawarma, Paneer Shawarma, Burgers, Waffles, Crunchy Chicken, and more. This menu expansion demonstrates their commitment to being a one-stop solution for food lovers, catering to various tastes and preferences. By continuously innovating and adding diverse options to their menu, Absolute Shawarma ensures that there is something for everyone, whether they crave traditional Middle Eastern flavors or are in the mood for a different culinary experience. Their dedication to quality and variety not only attracts a wide array of customers but also sets them apart in the competitive food industry.
